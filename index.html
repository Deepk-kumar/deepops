How quickly can you start on a project?
+
I can typically start within 48–72 hours of project confirmation and payment. For urgent cases (production is down, critical deadline), I can often start same-day. Book a free call and let's discuss your timeline.
Do you work with startups or only enterprises?
+
Both! I've worked with everything from 2-person startups to enterprises running 86+ AWS environments. My pricing is structured so startups can afford proper DevOps from day one — no need to wait until you're VC-funded.
What if something breaks after you hand over the project?
+
Every project includes 30 days of post-delivery support at no extra cost. If something I built breaks within that period, I fix it free of charge. After 30 days, I offer affordable retainer packages for ongoing support.

+
Absolutely — and I strongly prefer this approach. I document everything, explain decisions in plain English, and can do knowledge-transfer sessions with your team. My goal is to make you self-sufficient, not dependent on me forever.

Yes, always. I work under formal contracts with clear scope, timelines, payment terms, and NDA if required. Professional engagement only — no verbal agreements.
